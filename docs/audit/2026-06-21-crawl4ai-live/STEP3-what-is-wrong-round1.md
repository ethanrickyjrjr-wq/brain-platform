## Net (round 1)
- **Right:** the core stealth wiring (#1), the advanced dispatcher (#7), `CacheMode.BYPASS` (#10). The hardest part — the UndetectedAdapter strategy assembly — matches the doc exactly.
- **Wrong / fix:** the blind-`delay_after` waits (#5), the 8-minute `page_timeout` unit bug (#6), the missing `crawl4ai-doctor` on 4 jobs (#4), redundant 4-spelling installs that 0.9.0's `crawl4ai-setup` now collapses (#3), no jitter (#8), no streaming (#9), the moot litellm rationale (#11), and supercrawl4ai shipped-but-unused (#12).
- **Round 2 will fill:** the dispatcher/rate-limiter exact signatures, hooks-auth (the `after_goto` anti-bot gate), proxy-security (the only datacenter-IP lever), identity/session (warm-session anti-bot), content-selection + fit_markdown (denoise), virtual-scroll + table-extraction (Crexi + deterministic tables).
